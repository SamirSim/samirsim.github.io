École Normale Supérieure de Lyon
Performance Evaluation of Networks

Course Level: Master 1 (Bac+4)

Performance evaluation covers many topics, from theory to practice, in computer science and computer engineering. It includes the design of mathematical models (very often with probabilistic assumptions), the analysis of such models (mathematically or using simulation), the design of experimental protocols (measuring tools and data collection), the analysis of data (statistics on real experiments or in silico experiments). It is used to design, compare or tune computer systems and communication networks.


Teaching volume: 28h.
Course Responsible: Éric Thierry.
