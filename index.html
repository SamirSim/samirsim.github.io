About

I'm an Associate Professor at University of Lorraine. I conduct my research at the CRAN lab (ARN team, MPSI department), and my teachings at Polytech Nancy. Prior to that, I spent 2 years at the ICube lab of University of Strasbourg (France) as a Postdoc. I graduated as a PhD in Computer Science from ENS de Lyon (France) in 2023, and as a Computer Science Engineer from ESI Algiers (Algeria) in 2020. I held several visiting positions at the University of Waterloo (Canada), University of Malaga (Spain), Pusan National University (South Korea). My research interests include Digital Twins, 5G/6G Networks, IoT, Network Simulation and Performance Evaluation.
